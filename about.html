What is MyETHR?

My Expense to Hourly Ratio (MyETHR) is a simple yet powerful tool that helps you understand how long you need to work to afford something. By factoring in your hourly wage and state taxes, it gives you a clear picture of the effort required to make a purchase.

Why Was It Created?

Built by an immigrant healthcare worker, MyETHR was designed to make budgeting relatable and accessible. It is perfect for freelancers, students, and anyone who wants to make smarter financial decisions.

How Does It Work?

Simply enter your expense, hourly wage, and state. MyETHR will calculate the number of hours you need to work to afford it, taking into account average state income tax rates.

Who’s Behind It?

MyETHR was created by @franzescuzar, a newbie developer with a passion for creating tools that simplify everyday life.
